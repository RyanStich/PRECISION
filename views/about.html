about our company

Precision Appraisal Group is a full service appraisal company established in 2013 by Mike Mayhew, who began his appraisal career in 2006. Our primary focus is on residential, insurance, legal and assessment appeal assignments and we have a proven track record in all. Mike Mayhew BCom, AACI Mike began his professional career as a Canadian Military Search and Rescue helicopter pilot. Upon retiring from this rewarding experience, Mike began his appraisal career in 2006 with a strong background in business (BCom) and a Post Graduate Certificate in Real Property Valuation from UBC.
